Controls
	W - Move forward
	A - Strafe left
	S - Move backwards
	D - Strafe right
	Spacebar - Variable (See Below)
	Left Shift - Variable (See Below)
	Left Mouse Button - Sprint
	Mouse - Look

F1 - Enable Survival Mode
	Gravity, Collision detection, Universe Edge
	Spacebar - Jump
	Left Shift - None

F2 - Enable Creative Mode (Default)
	Creative Flight
	Spacebar - Move upwards
	Left Shift - Move downwards

F3 - Generate Overworld (Default)
	River Generation, Tree and Cactus Spawning

F4 - Generate Nether
	Lava Generation, Nether Portal, Glowstone and Netherquartz Spawning
